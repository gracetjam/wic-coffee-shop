About Company Page

WIC Coffee began as a childhood adventure among grandma’s fruit trees, including the lush and towering liberica coffee trees. The vibrant red hues of the coffee cherries, the unique appearance of the green beans, and the captivating aroma of the roast inspired a deep love for coffee. From espresso machines, coffee grinders, to modified roasting machines, everything became part of the journey to create better coffee.

From a handful of coffee beans, WIC Coffee grew into a small café in a suburban area, eventually expanding to other locations such as Coffee Lover in Ultimo, WIC Cafe in World Square Complex, and Micro Cart Coffee in Crown Casino. With unwavering dedication, WIC Coffee has earned prestigious awards, including Australian Barista Champion, Coffee Art Champion, and Best Coffee Award in the SMH Good Cafe Guide (2017-2019).

However, the COVID-19 pandemic brought new challenges, with a 60% drop in sales due to social restrictions. WIC Coffee continues to adapt, maintaining its quality and commitment to delivering the best coffee experience to its customers.
